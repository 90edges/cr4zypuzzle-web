About The Cr4zy Puzzle series

"The Cr4zy Puzzle" is a series of unusual applications for Mac OS X. Every application in the series should provide unique experience with relatively hard challenge to conquer. This series is not for everyone but it will, for sure, find a small group of fans sooner or later.
